### [Local First devroom at FOSDEM'26 conference in Brussels: the program is out!](https://openlocalfirst.org/)
That's your gift for Christmas edition: an 8 hours lineup full of the best projects, frameworks, talks, and speakers! 22 talks, by 25 speakers. You can't miss the first ever Local-First devroom at the upcoming [FOSDEM](https://fosdem.org/2026/) (biggest Free and Open Source conference in Europe). It will happen on the **1st of February 2026**. If you are into local first apps or frameworks, sync protocols, CRDT libraries or UX, join us in person or remotely, there will be a live streaming. For those coming to Brussels, we will have an after party with drinks just after the devroom concluded. more info here [openlocalfirst.org](https://openlocalfirst.org)! See you soon!
